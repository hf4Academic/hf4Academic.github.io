    Education and Experience

      I'm an associate professor at CNIC, CAS. Before joining CNIC, I was a senior algorithm expert at Kuaishou and an algorithm expert at Alibaba. I was also a Post-doc, jointly sponsored with Tsinghua and Alibaba. I recieved my PhD (Advisor: Youjian Zhao, Co-Advisor: Dan Pei) and bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University in 2017 and 2012.
